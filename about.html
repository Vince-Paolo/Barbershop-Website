Our Story

One chair, then a crew.

Faded & Co. started in 2018 as a single chair tucked into the back of a tailor shop in Calamba. Word got around about the line-ups, and within a year the chair became four. Today it's a full crew that still treats every cut the way the first one was treated — like it actually matters.

02 Mission & Vision

Mission

Give every client a precise, unrushed cut and a chair experience worth coming back for — no upsells, no shortcuts, just the work done right.

Vision

To be the barbershop Calamba sends visitors to first — known not for being the biggest shop, but the one that never lets a cut leave the chair unfinished.
